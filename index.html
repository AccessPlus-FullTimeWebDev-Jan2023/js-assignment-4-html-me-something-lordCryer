My Trip To India

My journey up into the Himalayian Mountains

This page decribes a few highlights of my trip to India which I took last year.
The first few days of my trip were spent high in the Himalaya Mountains.
It was very beautiful up there and the air was fresh and clear.
It was awesome waking up above the clouds every morning.
I spent several hours each day in meditation.

Learning the Culture

I met many different people and experienced their culture.
While I was there I learned how to prepare a traditional indian meal.
During my time there I also observed several religious ceremonies where we sang songs and listened to an Master speak.

My Final Adventure

During the last 2 days of my trip to India, I took a road trip from the Barala india to Agra India.
The host family that I stayed with and I rented a large suv with a driver and we were on our way.
The trip was long but well worth it when we arrived at the Taj Mahal!
